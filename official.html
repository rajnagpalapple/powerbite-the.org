Myrrh

Myrrh is a gum resin extracted from the Commiphora tree. Historically, it has been used for medicinal purposes, incense, and even perfumes. In oral care, its antimicrobial properties are beneficial for treating tooth infections and inflammations.

Wild Mint

Wild Mint is a herb renowned for its medicinal value. In PowerBite, the mint leaves contribute to cleaning the mouth and offering a refreshing taste. Its antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties also help protect teeth from decay.

Clove Oil

Clove Oil is an essential oil with a long history of use as a remedy for toothaches and other oral health problems. Its active ingredient, eugenol, has anesthetic effects, which can help numb parts of the mouth, reducing pain from issues like toothaches.
5

Xylitol

Xylitol is a naturally occurring sugar alcohol found in many fruits and vegetables. It's often used as a sugar substitute and has been linked to a reduced risk of cavities and better overall oral health.
6

Lysozyme

Lysozyme is an enzyme known for its effects on immunity. It plays a crucial role in innate immunity, helping to protect the body against bacteria, viruses, and fungi by attacking the protective cell walls of certain bacteria.

Mediterranean Sea Salt

Mediterranean Sea Salt is often used in saltwater mouthwashes to support oral health. Rinsing the mouth with saltwater has been shown to be an effective cleansing solution, especially for gum infections, by drawing out excess fluid in infected tissues.

Ashwagandha (mentioned in the context of stress hormone cortisol)

Myrhh
